About us

Concept

Food Recommendation is the leading destination for customer ratings and reviews of products. Consumers find ratings and read reviews to ensure they buy from the best companies.

Idea

Because « 85 % of consumers trust online reviews as much as personal recommendations » – Bright Local.
These statistics and your personal experience should bring you to the conclusion that a web review service is in high demand now and can be a driving link in creating a chain between customers and businesses.

Reviews

The review should be based on your own experience and that you are in no way affiliated with this business, No incentive or payment has been provided from any company to write your opinion..

Rules

All false review constitutes a violation of the terms of use of our platform ,This means that any attempt to fake reviews can lead to legal action against you. So be careful

Recipes

Share your product experiences to help others make better decisions and empower businesses to improve.

Goal

Our site is a community of passionate people, sharing their products and service experiences. Our community of reviewers are a dedicated bunch of consumers who want to help you discover what's right for you.
